Welcome to Happy Tails!
Because Every Pet Deserves a Frame in the Heart!


Our mission is to immortalize the joy, love, and loyalty they bring into your life through the artistry of photography.

From the mischievous twinkle in their eyes to the softest fur textures, we aim to capture it all.

Our photography sessions are designed to create a comfortable and stress-free environment for your pets.
Whether we're chasing tails in your backyard, exploring favorite parks, or capturing the quiet moments at home, our goal is to showcase the true spirit of your pet in their natural element.

Every pet is unique, and so should be their portraits.

What We Offer

At Happy Tails studio, we offer a range of services tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of our clients:

  • Customized pet portrait sessions
  • On-location or studio photoshoots
  • Specialized sessions for multiple pets or specific events
  • Professional Editing and Post-Processing
